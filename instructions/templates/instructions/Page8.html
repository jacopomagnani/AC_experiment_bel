Every time a partnership is formed between you and another player, you earn an amount of points that depends only on the type of the other player. If no partnership is formed between you and the other player, you earn an amount of points that depends only on your own type. The tables below show you the actual amounts of points you can earn. If a partnership is formed between you and an X type, you earn 160 points. If a partnership is formed between you and a Y type, you earn 80 points. If a partnership is formed between you and a Z type, you earn 40 points. If no partnership is formed between you and the other player and your type is X, you earn 100 points. If no partnership is formed between you and the other player and your type is Y, you earn 75 points. If no partnership is formed between you and the other player and your type is Z, you earn 25 points.
