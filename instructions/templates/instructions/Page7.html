FORMING A PARTNERSHIP
{% endblock %} {% block content %}

When you are an active player, after you have received a clue about your potential partner’s type, you can decide whether you want to form a partnership or not


The computer will decide on behalf of the passive player whether to form a partnership or not.


Only if both the active player and the computer acting on the passive player’s behalf choose to form a partnership, then a partnership is formed. For example, if you are an active player and choose to form a partnership but the computer of the passive player chooses not to, the partnership is not formed.
